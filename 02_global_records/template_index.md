## Template product criteria

A template product should:

1. solve a clear repeated problem
2. be directly usable
3. reduce repeated work
4. include examples, fields, or steps
5. connect to free content or a free tool
6. be possible to extend into consulting, service, or product functionality

## Maintenance rule

Update this index when:

- a new template product project is created
- product scope changes
- a product is launched
- a product is paused
- a product validates a future feature hypothesis
